FAQ

Which Apple Watch models work?

Apple Watch Ultra 2, Series 9, or newer with watchOS 10+. These support the 800Hz sensors needed for accurate analysis.

Why HealthKit access?

Required to create workout sessions for high-frequency sensor access. We don't read your health data.

What is Test Mode?

Uses simulated data to demo the app without swinging.

How is tempo calculated?

Backswing time ÷ downswing time. Golf ideal: 3:1. Tennis: 2:1. Baseball: 2.5:1.

"Motion Access Required"?

Go to Watch app → Settings → Privacy → Motion & Fitness. Enable for SwingLab Pro.
