Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2024

Overview

SwingLab Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

SwingLab Pro collects the following data locally on your device:

  • Motion Data: Accelerometer and gyroscope data during swing sessions for analysis.
  • Workout Data: Basic workout session info through HealthKit to enable sensor access.
  • Swing History: Your analysis results, scores, and timestamps.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit data to servers, share with third parties, use for advertising, or store in the cloud.

HealthKit

We use HealthKit to create workout sessions for sensor access. We do not read or write any health data.

Data Deletion

Uninstall the app to delete all locally stored data.
